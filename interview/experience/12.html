TCS Placement Drive Experience

First of all i would like you to know there were 4 rounds overall in TCS-
1) Online Aptitude 2) Technical Interview 3) Managerial Interview 4) HR 1))
Almost 650 students appeared for Online Aptitude and 306 was selected for next rounds ,
1st day only aptitude was there and they declared in midnight only.
In this round you will get 1 E-mail writing(10 min) then 30 aptitude questions (80 min)
2)) Technical Interview As i am from CS background so they asked me from C, C++, S,DBMS From C++ (Polymorphism, Inheritance, Difference b/w C C++, Function overloading etc...)
From C they asked some questions also asked to write fibonacci program, and string pallindrome etc. It was a relaxing round as they will make you sit in a comfort zone.
3) Next was Managerial interview-- These are generally arranged to see how much stress you can handle....so they asked me about cloud computing, then models of Software development nd some other questions...
4) Lastly they called me for HR interview- This was just a formality..nd it was just for 2 min.
They asked me about flexibility to move in any part of india and to work in any shifts , and also about bonds of 2 year..thats all.
Lastly result announced and totally 242 were selected and i was in one of them.
