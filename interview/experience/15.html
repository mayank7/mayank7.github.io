and yes dont forget to use every keywords its mandatory

time n work pnc probability geomectry 2 questions were from logical reasoning olso.
2 questions were repeated from open seesame also.. therefore, i suggest you people to be through with questions from openseesame and a big doubt about the cut off of apti

They basically judge your accuracy. So do not attempt the questions you are not confirm about.
Now, comes the technical interview... it was basically surrounded to these areas- final year project, summer training and as I am from Electronics field few questions were based on my fav. subject and a few from c language as i mentioned c only in my resume.
after my interview finished and i came out i was pretty confident about the selection in the techinterview.
Now, comes the HR interview:
questions were related to general issues, role of it in social media, justification on strengths, tell me in 3 words how your friends would describe you, an idea you could present for the welfare of society.
And finally interviews finished and my name was called out among the few odd students selected.
practice as many questions you can twice thrice as many times coz the questions might repeat or digits might also get changed but questions are almost same.
. Be confident..
coz its the biggest strength..
