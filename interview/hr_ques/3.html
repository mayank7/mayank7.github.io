What is the meaning of your name?
(Not important, but you should be aware of it, sometimes asked as 'filler' during interview)

Are you overqualified for this position?
(The obvious answer is NO, you should answer trying to match your skills with the job)

Explain how would be an asset to this organization?
(Again explain how your skills, achievements matches the job and can contribute to company)

Who has inspired you in your life and why?
(Start with your Parents and end up tying your teachers, role model etc)

What was the toughest decision you ever had to make?
(Can't say anything, you should think about it)

How do you define success and how do you measure up to your own definition?
(Satisfying myself and others who are realted to me or I am related to and feeling that I am spending my life doing something which I love to do and excelling in that field)

In order to achieve your goals, will you adopt unfair means ?
(Obviously NO, because there cannot be a correct path to do incorrect things)

How does the past help you in facing the future?
(Provides maturity, experience, better decision making capabilities etc)

What is God? Do you believe in Him ?
(For those who believe in God, He can be called as the Creator of universe with his supernatural powers, maintaining a perfect balance of everything under his control, a faith in everyone's heart that there is someone who is with everyone, eveready to help without any partiality, the solution to every problem)

On a scale of one to ten, rate yourself.
(The most trusted, accepted and safe answer is 7 (because it is neither too low nor too high, acceptable). However you can have your own.)
