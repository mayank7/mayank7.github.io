• Use proper resume format, TCS resume format is the simplest and the best resume format.

• Avoid poor ways of presentation.

• Don't put unnecessary details.

• Give proper and precise details.

• Don't make your resume too lengthy, it should be of 2-3 pages.

• Mention only those subjects for which you are well prepared. Don't put subjects of just introductory knowledge.

• Avoid decorations, don't make your resume look like a Greeting card.

• Put your contents in resume in accordance to the type of organization/company you are going for interview.

• Resume and Curriculum Vitae are similiar but not exactly same, keep in mind the difference between these two.
