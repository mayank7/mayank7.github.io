31) (a% of a)+(b% of b)=2% of ab then what % of a is b?

Soln:
(a% of a)+(b% of b)=2% of ab
ie a^2 + b^2 =2ab
ie (a-b)^2 =0
ie a-b=0
a=b
ie 100%.
