27) ((77!(77!-2*54!)^3)/(77!+54!)^3)+(54!((2*77!-54!)^3)/(77!+54!)^3)= ?

Soln:
assume a=77!& b=54!
according to the expression
(a(a-2b)^3)/(a+b)^3 + (b(2a-b)^3)/(a+b)^3)
=(a^4+2*a^3*b -2*a*b^3-b^4)/(a+b)^3
= (a-b)*(a+b)^3/(a+b)^3
= (a-b)
= (77!-54!)

28) Find the probability that a leap year chosen at random has 53 sundays?

Soln:
A leap year has 366 days, therefore 52 weeks i.e. 52 Sunday and 2 days.
The remaining 2 days may be any two days of the week.
For having 53 Sundays in a year, one of the remaining 2 days must be a Sunday.
n(S) = 7
n(E) = 2
P(E) = n(E) / n(S) = 2 / 7
