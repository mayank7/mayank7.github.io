83) What is the remainder of expression 48 ^ 567 /7 ?

Soln:
48 ^ 567 / 7
= (7*7-1)^567 / 7
= (-1)^567 / 7
= -1/7
rem = 7-1 = 6

= 3^(10k+8)*10^(10k+8)/11
= 3^10k * 3^8 * 10^(10k+8)/11
= (3^5)^2k * 3^5*3^3 * 10^(10k+8)/11
= (11*22+1)^2k *(11*22+1)*(11*2+5)*(11*1-1)^(10k+8)/ 11
=> rem = 1^2k * 1 * 5 *(-1)^(10k+8) = 5
