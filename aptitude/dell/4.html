Applying pythagoras theorem, diameter is 10cm.

32) Instead of walking along two adjacent sides of a rectangular field, a boy took a short cut along the diagonal and saves a distance equal to half the longer side. Then find the ratio of the shorter side to the longer side ?

Soln:
ratio of sides =3/4
if sides are 3x and 4x
distance while walking on side = 3x+4x=7x
distance on diagonal = 5x
distance saved = 2x which is half of 4x.

33) A single pipe of diameter x has to be replaced by six pipes of diameters 10 cm each. The pipes are used to convey some liquid in a laboratory. If the speed/flow of the liquid is maintained the same then the value of x is ?

Soln:
Volume of water flowing through 1 pipe of dia x = Volume discharged by 6 pipes of dia 6 cms
As speed is same, area of cross sections should be same.
Area of bigger pipe of dia x = Total area of 6 smaller pipes
pi*R^2 = 6 * pi* 5^2
R = 12.25 cm
Dia = 24.5 cm
