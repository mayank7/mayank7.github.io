1) If the radius of a circle is increased by 20% then the area is increased by?

Soln:
Radius is increased by 20% so new r=1.2r, New area=1.44pi(r^2) increase in area .. 0.44* 100 = 44%

3) 600 people are there.10 benches fell short so on each bench 2 extra members were made to sit. find the no. of benches ?

Soln:
Let no of benches= x.
Benches required to seat 600 people =x+10.
No of people per bench = 600/(x+10).
Now, 10 benches short. no of people per bench= [600/(x+10)]+2= 600/x.
x = 50

4) The amount of water (in ml) that should be added to reduce 9 ml lotion, containing 50% alcohol, to a lotion containing 30% alcohol, is ?

Soln:
50% of 9ml = 30% of x ml. so, x= 15ml.
Now, amount of water to be added= 15-9= 6ml, as alcohol remains same.

9) A cube of side 12 cm is painted red on all the faces and then cut into smaller cubes, each of side 3 cm. What is the total number of smaller cubes having none of their faces painted?

Soln:
Volume of 12 cm Cube (L^3) = Total Number of 3 cm cubes (say N) * Volume of 3 cm Cube
12*12*12 = N*3*3*3
Solving we get N= 4*4*4 = 64
12 cm cube when broken makes up 4 pices of 3 cm cubes on each side.
Only cubes at the core would not be painted. These cubes could be identified easily by removing equivalent
number of smaller cubes which form the outer surface of bigger cube.
Visualize a structure of 3 cms cubes kept in 4*4*4 structure forming 12cm cube.
Total number of cubes forming outer surface = Total number of cube faces in outer surface - 2* total number of cube at the corners with three shared faces - total number of shared cubes at the edges with 2 shared faces.
Total number of 3 cms cubes faces which are part of 6 faces of 12 cm cube = 16 * 6 = 96
3 faces of cubes which are at the corner would be shared along 3 faces, number of such cubes = 8
2 faces of cubes which are towards the edge but are not corners would be shared along 2 faces, number of such cubes = 6*4 = 24 Total number of cubes forming outer surface = 16*6 - 2*8 -6*4 = 56 cubes
Number of cubes with unpainted edges = Total Number of 3 cm cubes - Total number of cubes forming outer surface = 64 - 56 = 8 cubes
