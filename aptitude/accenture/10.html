93) In a classroom the average height of all the boys was 170 cm. Rahim recently joine the class which originally had 20 boys increased the average to 171cm. Then find the height of Rahim ?

Soln:
Before Rahim joined the total height of all the boys = 170 X 20 = 3400.
New average = (Total height of all students + height of rahim)/21 = 180
Therefore (3400 + height of rahim)/21 = 171
Therefore height of Rahim = 3780 - 3400 = 191 cms.

94) A hat vendor bought hats at Rs 5 per 3 hats. He sold them at Rs 10 per 4 hats. What was his profit percentage ?

Soln:
Cp of one hat=5/3
Sp of one hat=10/4
profit=10/4-5/3=5/6
profit%=profit/cp*100
5/6/5/3*100=50%

95) A 20 litre solution conatins oil and kerosene in the ratio 3:5,replace 4 litres of mixture with 4 litres of kerosene what will be the ratio of oil and kerosene?

Soln:
When 4 liters are taken out of 20ml, amount of oil in remaining 16 lt = 3/8 * 16 = 6
Hence the remaining 10 litres would be kerosene
Now additional 4 litres of kerosene is added to the solution
So total quantity of kerosene = 14 litres
Ratio of oil:kerosene = 6/14 = 3/7.
