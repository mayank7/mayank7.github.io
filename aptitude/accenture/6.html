54) 10 men can complete a piece of work in 15 days and 15 women can complete the same work in 12 days. If all the 10 men and 15 women work together, in how many days will the work get completed?

Soln:
In 1 day, 10 men can do 1/15 of the work and 15 women can do 1/12 of the work, so in 1 day working together they would do 1/15 + 1/12 of the work i.e. 3/20 of the work.
So it would take 6 days and two-thirds of 7th day.

56) (3X+2)(2X-5)=AX^2+ KX+N; then what is the value of A-N+K?

Soln:
(3x+2)(2x-5)=6x^2-11x-10
So,A=6, K=-11, N=-10
Finally,
A-N+K=6-(-10)+(-11)
=6+10-11
=5

58) Silu and Meenu were walking on the road.Silu said, "I weigh 51 Kgs. How much do you weigh?"Meenu replied that she wouldn't reveal her weight directly as she is overweight. But she said, "I weigh 29 Kgs plus half of my weight."How much does Meenu weigh?

Soln:
It is given that Meenu weighs 29 Kgs plus half of her own weight.
It means that 29 Kgs is the other half. So she weighs 58 Kgs.
Solving mathematically, let's assume that her weight is X Kgs.
X = 29 + X/2
2*X = 58 + X
X = 58 Kgs

59) Consider the sum: ABC + DEF + GHI = JJJ .If different letters represent different digits, and there are no leading zeros, what does J represent?

Soln:
The value of J must be 9. Since there are no leading zeros, J must be 7, 8, or 9. (JJJ = ABC + DEF + GHI= 14? + 25? + 36? = 7??)
Now, the remainder left after dividing any number by 9 is the same as the remainder left after dividing the sum of the digits of that number by 9.
Also, note that 0 + 1 + ... + 9 has a remainder of 0 after dividing by 9 and JJJ has a remainder of 0, 3, or 6.
The number 9 is the only number from 7, 8 and 9 that leaves a remainder of 0, 3, or 6 if you remove it from the sum 0 + 1 + ... + 9.
Hence, it follows that J must be 9.

Soln:
