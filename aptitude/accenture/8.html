76) A train runs first half of the distance at 40 km/hr and the remaining half at 60 km/hr. What is the average speed for the entire journey?

Soln:
In these cases, if two speeds are x and y, then
average speed = 2*x*y/(x+y)
so here av speed = 2*40*60/100 = 48 km/hr
