112) In a 2 digit no unit's place is halved and tens place is doubled. diff bet the nos is 37.digit in unit's place is 2 more than tens place?

Soln:
Let tenth place digit be x & unit place be y
then no. is = 10x + y
if unit place is halved and tens place is doubled
then no. is = 10*2x + y/2
10*2x + y/2 -(10x+y)=37
=> 10x - y/2 = 37 .....(1)
and, y-x=2 ....(2)
solving, x=4,y=6
no. is 46.

114) one man can do a piece of work in 10days. one boys join him & he complete the work in 6days . if he gets 50rs for his work then what will be the share of the boy ?

Soln:
1 day work of Man=1/10, If Boy's 1 day work=1/x, then
(1/10)+(1/x)=1/6, x=15
Ratio of work of Man & Boy=1/10:1/15=3:2
So out of 50 Rs. Boy's share=2*50/(3+2)=20

118) A rectangular tank can hold k litres of water.How many litres of water can tank hold if all dimensions doubles ?

Soln:
let dimensions of tank are x*y*z=xyz
so xyz can hold k litres of water
now dimensions are doubled i.e 2x*2y*2z=8xyz
as xyz holds k litres,so 8xyz will hold 8*k=8k litres of water.
