Soln:
Sum = (n/2)(2a + (n - 1)d)
(20/2)(2a + 19d)= (30/2)(2a + 29d)
20a + 190d = 30a + 435d
10a = - 245d
a =( - 49/2) d
Sum of 50 first terms = 25(-49d +49d)= 0

3) A garrison had provision for a certain number of days. After 10 days, 1/5 of the men desert and it is found that the provisions will now last just as long as before. How long was that?

Soln:
1/5th of the men are satisfied for 10 days
Implies 4/5 th of the men will be satisfied for 4*10 = 40 days.
So, total days = 50 days

6) 2 ^ 51 = X, last digit of X is ?

Soln:
last digit of 2's power: 2,4,8,6.
After 12 iterations, the third digit is 8 (12*4 + 3 = 51)
So, answer is 8.

10) My mother gave me money to buy stamps of price 2paisa, 7 paisa,15 paisa, 10paisa and 20 paisa. I had to buy 5 each of three types and 6 each of the other 2 types .
But on my way to the post office i forgot how many of stamps of each type were to be brought . My mother had given me rupees 3 . So i had no problem in finding out the exact amount of each one .
Can you tell me which stamps were 5 in number , n whic were 6 in number ?

Soln:
5 each of three types and 6 each of two types.
So, three types are 2, 7, 15 paisa stamps.
Amount is 2*5 + 7*5 + 15*5 =120 paisa.
Two types are 10, 20 paisa stamps.
Amount is 10*6 + 20*6 =180.
TOtal amount is 300 paisa i.e., 3 rupees.
