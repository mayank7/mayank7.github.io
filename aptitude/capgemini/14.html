132) In a 100m race, B bet C by 25m. A bet B by 20m. Then A will beat C by what metres ?

Soln:
b/c=100/(100-25)=4/3
a/b=100/(100-20)=5/4
a/c=(a/b)*(b/c)=5/3=100/60
so a beat c by (100-60)=40m
