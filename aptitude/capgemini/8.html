80) A certain money market account that had a balance of $48,000 during all of last month earned $360 in interest for the month. At what simple annual interest rate did the account earn interest last month?

Soln:
rate = SI*100/(P*T)
here, P=48000, SI=360, T=1/12
thereofore r = 360*100/(48000*(1/12)) = 9%
