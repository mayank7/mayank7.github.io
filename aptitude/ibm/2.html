13) One person traveled 4 laps with the speed of 10,20,40 ( or 30) and 60 kmph and what is the average speed ?

Soln:
Let distance covered in one lap be x
therefore total distance covered= 4x
t1= x/10
t2= x/20
t3=x/30
t4=x/60
total time= t1+t2+t3+t4= 0.2x
avg speed= total distance covered/ total time taken=4x/0.2x= 20

speed upstream=speed of boat in still water - speed of stream
given, time is same
24/10+x=16/10-x

18) A woman goes to company 6 days in aweek the bus fare for her 1 day journey is 0.2$,and on every saturday she uses her car to go to office and the expenditure is 3$ how much will be rise of her total expenditure for a week if she uses car to go ?

Soln:
out of 6 days, 5 days she uses bus and 1 day car
so total expenditure= 5*0.2+3= 4$
if she will use car everyday then her expenditure will be 6*3= 18$
increase in expenditure= 18-4= 14$.
