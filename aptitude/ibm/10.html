95) A man reaches his office two hours late travelling at 50 km/hr.if he increases his speed to 60km/hr,he is late by 1 hour. Find the distance he has to travel to reach his office and speed required to reach the office in time?

Soln:
let distance be d km & time reqd be t hour then
d/50 = (t+2)
d/60 = (t+1)
=> 50(t+2)=60(t+1)
=> 5t+10=6t+6
=> t=4
put t=4 in eqn d/50=(t+2)=> d=50(4+2)=300
d = 300 km & t = 4 hour
Speed required to reach in time = 300/4 = 75 kms/hr.
