1) 135,486,422,224,140,541,134,431,145,596,421...find next three terms?

Soln:
Break the series into 3 parts, as 422 is reversed to 224, likewise 134 is reversed to 431.
Therefore, 421 is reversed to 124
135+5=140
140+145
therefore, 145+5=150
486+55=541
541+55=596
therefore,596+55=651
so the next three no.are 124,150,651

9) Some x no of bullets are shared by 3 people equally and each fired 4 bullets then the sum of remaining bullets is equal to initially division value ?

Soln:
x no of bullets divided between 3 people the each has x/3 no of bullets
by firing 4 bullets each has (x/3)-4 bullets
by adding all the remaining bullets of each is equal to the initial divison value so
(x/3-4)+(x/3-4)+(x/3-4)+(x/3-4)=x/3
x = 18.
