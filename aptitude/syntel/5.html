41) a+b+c=0, then roots of ax^2+bx+c=0 is?

Soln:
x(ax+b)+c=0
x(ax-a-c)+c=0
ax^2-ax-cx+c=0
ax(x-1)-c(x-1)=0
(ax-c)(x-1)=0
thus x=c/a and x=1 are the roots.

42) A man borrowed Rs.1000 at 5% per annum at simple interest.Each year he pays Rs.200 for interest and part of debt. The amount that is left to be paid back in the end of 3 years is ?

Soln:
P=1000
R=5%
T=3 yrs
S.I + Debt= 200 for 3 years i.e (S.I+Debt) at end of 3 yrs= 200 x 3= 600
S.I=(1000 x 5 x 3)/ 100= 150
Amount= 1000+150=1150
Already paid= 600 (S.I + Debt)
Amount left to be paid = 1150-600=550.

43) If A can do a work in 12 days, B in 24 days and C in 6 days, then how many days will they take to finish the job together?

Soln:
A's one day work = 1/12
B's one day work = 1/24
C's one day work = 1/6
A, B and C's one day work = 1/12 + 1/24 + 1/6 = 7/24.
Time taken by A, B and C's together to finish their work = 24/7 Days
