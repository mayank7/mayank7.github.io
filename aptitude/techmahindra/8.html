71) In a company there are 5 partners A,B,C,D,E. They invested money in the ratio of 1:2:3:4:5. If after one year if there is a profit of Rs.15,000. Then share of E will be ?

Soln:
Total profit = 15000
share of E = Profit*ratio(E)/(ratio(A)+ratio(B)+ratio(C)+ratio(D)+ratio(E))
= 15000*5/(1+2+3+4+5)=5000.

74) In a game of 80 points; A can give B 5 points and C 15 points. Then how many points B can give C in a game of 60 ?

Soln:
B/C= (B/A)*(A/C)= (75/80)*(80/65)=15/13
B/C=15/13= 15*4/13*4= 60/52
In a game of 60, B can give C 8 points.
