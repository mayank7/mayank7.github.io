so, B:C=420:399
so, in the game of 420, B gives "21" (420-399) points to C..
so, in the game of 500, B gives to C as

37) Some men can do a work in 10 days. with 10 less than original no of men it takes 10 more days to complete the work. what is the original no of men?

Soln:
let the no. of men be x
x men can do in 10 days,so one man can do in 10*x days
now (x-10) men can do in (10+10=20 days),so one man can do in 20*(x-10)days
10x=20(x-10)
or x=2x-20
or x=20 men.
