81) Of two numbers, 4 times the smaller one is less than 3 times the 1arger one by 5. If the sum of the numbers is larger than 6 times their difference by 6, find the two numbers ?

Soln:
If smaller number=x, larger number = y 3y-5=4x or 3y-4x=5 ---(i) &
x+y=6(y-x)+6 or 5y-7x=-6 ---(ii)
Solving (i) & (ii), x=43, y=59.

83) If a^2+b^2+2b+4a+5=0 then what is the value of a-b/a+b ?

Soln:
a^2+b^2+2b+4a+5=0
=> (a^2+4a+4)+(b^2+2b+1)= 0
=> (a+2)^2 + (b+1)^2 = 0
=> a+2 = 0 & b+1 = 0
=> a = -2 & b = -1
a-b/a+b = -2+1 / -2-1 = 1/3.
