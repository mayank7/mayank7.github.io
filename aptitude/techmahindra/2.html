11) If the radius of a circle is increased by 12%, then the area of the circle increases by what percent ?

Soln:
Let radius be x
A1=pi*x^2
A2=pi*(x+x*12/100)^2=pi*(1.12x)^2
so area increases
increase in area=(A2-A1)/A1 *100=pi*(1.12+1)*(1.12-1)*x^2/pi*x^2 *100
=2.12*0.12/100 = 25.44%.

12) The perimeter of a square and a rectangle is the same. If the rectangle is 12 cms. by 10 cms., then by what percentage is the area of the square more than that of the rectangle?

Soln:
perimeter of rectangle=2*(12+10)=44=perimeter of square
so side of square=44/4=11
area of rectangle=12*10=120
area of square=11*11=121
reqd%=(121-120)/120 *100=100/120=5/6.
