51) 6 women can do 75 unit of work in 8 days by working 5hr/day, in how many days 4 women do 30 units of work by working 8hr/day ?

Soln:
Use (man*days*hours/work).
Thus m1*d1*hi/w1 = m2*d2*h2/w2.
Hence d2 is 3 days.

52) 12*18*6 is the volume of some material. How many cubes of edge 3 can be insert into it?

Soln:
no. of such cubes = volume of material / volume of one cube
(12*18*6)/(3*3*3)= 48

54) A and B are 90 km apart they start to move each other simuntaneously A at speed 10 n B at 5k/hr if every hour they double their speed. What is the distance that A pass until he meets B ?

Soln:
relative distance b/w A and B =90 km
After 2 hrs A's speed is 20 km/hr and B's speed is 10 km/hr
relative speed after 2 hrs is 30 km/hr
relative time=90/30=3 hrs
so after 3 hrs they will meet each other
and A covers 20*3=60 kms.

55) At a special sale 5 ticket can be purchased as a price of 3 ticket, if 5 ticket are purchased at the sale, the amount saved will be what % of the original price of the 5 ticket ?

Soln:
Let the price of 1 ticket be Rs.10
Original C.P. of 5 tickets = Rs.50
After sale 5 Tickets were bought for Rs. 30
%amount saved = ((50-30)/50)*100 = 40%


56) If 4 consecutive letters are selected at random from the English alphabet, then the probability that one of the letters is a vowel is ?

Soln:
Total no. of ways of selecting 4 consecutive letters => 23 [Like abcd, bcde, efgh,..etc]
No. of ways of having one is a vowel => abcd, bcde, cdef, defg, efgh, fghi, ghij, hijk, ijkl, lmno, mnop, nopq, opqr, rstu, stuv, tuvw, uvwx...
= 17
P(one of letter is vowel) = 17/23
