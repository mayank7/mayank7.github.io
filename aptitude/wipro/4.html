31) The local recycling plant had a contract requiring anyone who obtained recycled bottlesfor storage for them, to bring them back to be recycled again. The plant could make one newbottle from every seven bottles returned. One week, on Monday, they got 343 bottles torecycle. Assuming that everybody brought back all the empties, how many could they eventually remake from the 343?

Soln:
In 1st level they will make 343/7=49 bottles.
In 2nd level they will make 49/7=7 bottles.
In 3rd level they will make 7/7=1 bottle.
Totally they can remake 49+7+1=57 bottles.

Soln:
let the total number of candies be x,
Amar share = x/3,
babu share = x/4,
chetan share = x/5,
dinakar share = x/6,

35) Ten Rs.5.00 bills, ten Rs.10.00 bills and ten Rs.20.00 bills are in a box. A blind-folded personmust remove the bills from the box. The contest ends when a person removes three samebills(example: three Rs.5.00 bills). What is the most amount of money a person can removefrom the box?

Soln:
90rs maximum a person remove from a box.
First he remove 2 Rs.5 bills = 2* 5 = 10rs
Then he remove 2 Rs.10 bills = 2* 10 = 20rs
Then he remove 3 Rs.20 bills = 3* 20 = 60rs
So, totally he remove = 10+20+60 = 90rs.
