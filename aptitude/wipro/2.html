they can complete the work together in 1/(1/20 + 1/60) = 1/ (4/60) = 15 days.

a/c to the problem,

work left after 13 days = 1- 9/10 -1/12 = 1/10-1/12 = 1/60
1/60 work will be completed by B on 14 th day by taking quarter of a day.
so total 13 1/4 days.
