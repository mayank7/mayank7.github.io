21) A can do a piece of work in 20 days and B in 30 days. A starts the work and worked for 6 days. Then B completed the remaining part of the work. In how many days was the work completed?

Soln:
A one day work = 1/20
B one day work = 1/30
A 6 day work = 6/20
Let B works for x days
B x day work = x/30
Now since A and B complete the work
6/20+x/30=1
Which gives x as 21
Now total no. of days=21+6 = 27days.

25) The rectangle box with square base is open at the top. The maximum volume of the box made from 1200 m^2 tin,in m^3 is ?

Soln:
Let the length be x and the breadth be y.
Now since it has a square base;
x=y
Now the surface area can be given as S= x^2+4xy (Area of square base + Area of Sides)
And Volume can be given as V = x^2y
ATQ, S=1200,so 1200=X2+4xy
y=(1200-x2)/4x
Now for V to be max
V'=0
V'(x)=x2y
Solving for x we get x=20
Now y=20m
Hence volume is 20x20x10 = 4000m.
