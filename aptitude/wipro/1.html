1) X varies inversely as square of y. Given that y = 2 for x = 1. The value of x for y = 6 will be equal to ?

Soln:
x = k/y*y
When x is 1, y is 2
we have
1 = k/2*2
1*4 = k
4 = k
So, when y is 6
x = 4/36
x = 1/9.

2) If a person walks at 4/5th of his usual spee he reaches 40min late. If he walks at his usual speed how much time does he travels?

Soln:
Let his usual speed is x and time taken is t minutes,
so distance travelled is xt.
If his speed is (4/5)*x then time taken is (t+40) minutes to cover the same distance.
therefore (4/5)*x (t+40)= xt => t/5 = 160/5
=> t = 160 minutes.

3) A can copy 50 papers in 10 hrs, while A & B can copy 70 papers in 10 hrs. How many hours are required for B to copy 26 papers?

Soln:
A can copy 50 papers in 10 hrs, while A & B can copy 70 papers in 10 hrs.
It means B can copy 20 papers in 10 hrs.
Then time taken by B to copy 26 papers = 26*10/20 = 13 hours.

4) Twenty women can do a work in sixteen days. Sixteen men can complete the same work in fifteen days. What is the ratio between the capacity of a man and a woman?

Soln:
1 woman's one day work = 1/(16*20)
1 man's one day work = 1/(16*15)
ratio between the capacity of a man and a woman = 1/(16*20) : 1/(16*15)
3:4.

5) A can do a work in 12 days.When he had worked for 3 days B joined him. If they complete the work in 3 more days. In how many days can B alone can finish the work?

Soln:
Suppose B can complete the work in x days, then
A's 6 days' work = 6/12
B's 3 days' work = 3/x
then 6/12 + 3/x =1
3/x = 1/2
x = 6
B alone can finish the work in 6 days.
