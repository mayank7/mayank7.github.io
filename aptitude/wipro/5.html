41) It takes 30 days to fill a laboratory dish with bacteria. If the size of the bacteria doubles each day, how long did it take for the bacteria to fill one half of dish?

Soln:
When bacterias double their size from half dish to full dish in one day and fill dish in 30 days, it clearly means that on 29 th day, the dish was half full of bacteria.

42) There are two sections in a question paper each contain five questions. A student has to answer 6 questions. Maximum no. of questions that can be answered from any section is 4. How many ways he can attempt the paper ?

Soln:

44) A and B throw a dice. The probabilty that A's throw is not greater than B's is ?

Soln:
If b's outcome is 6, a's outcome can be 6,5,4,3,2,1.total=6
similarly if b's outcome is 5,a's outcome can be 5,4,3,2,1=5
so a's outcome which is lower then b is 6+5+4+3+2+1=21
and total outcome is 36 so our probability is 21/36 or 7/12.

47) A car is 250 meters behind the bus. The car and bus are moving with speed 60 km/hr and 35 km/hr respectively. The car will be ahead of bus by 250 meters in ?

Soln:
Relative speed=60-35=25 Km/hr
For car to be ahead of bus by 250 meter,distance to be covered=250+250=500m=0.5km
So time taken=0.5/25=1/50 Hr or 3600/50=72 seconds

48) Two trains each 100m long moving in opposite direction cross each other in 8 seconds if one is moving twice as fast as other then the speed of faster train is?

Soln:
Let the speed of slow train=x and fast train=2x
Distance covered=100+100=200 meters, with the speed=x+2x=3x
Time=200/3x=8 sec ->x=25/3 m/s
Therefore fast train speed=2x=50/3 m/s=50*3600/3*1000=60 km/hr

49) The difference between two numbers is one-seventh of the sum of these numbers two numbers. What is the ratio of the two numbers?

3x=4y
so the ratio will be 4:3

50) I read 3/8 of a book on one day, and 4/5 of the remainder on another day. If now there were 30 pages unread, the book contains ?

Soln:
Let x be total no. of pages in the book.
You read 3x/8 pages on 1st day and 5x/8*4/5=x/2 pages on another day.
Remaining no of pages=30.
(3x/8)+(x/2)+30=x
Therefore x=240.
