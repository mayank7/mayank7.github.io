12) An express train takes 3 seconds to enter tunnel which is 1 km long. If it is traveling at 120 km an hour, how long will it take to pass completely through the tunnel ?

Soln:
speed = 120km/hr
enter a tunnel,, means that the train covers a distance equal to its own length
so (120*5/18)=x/3 where x= length of train
hence x= 100 mtrs
to pass completely train has to travel a distance =(x + tunnel length) that is 1100mtrs
so (1100/t)=(120*5)/18
t= 33 sec
