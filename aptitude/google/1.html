1) What is the sum of the product and quotient of 8 and 8?

Soln:
Product = 8*8 = 64
Quotient = 8/8 = 1
Sum of product and quotient = 64 + 1 = 65

7) Population of a colony of ants is increasing by 20% per day. If the population on Monday was 3000, on which day of that week the population would be 5184 ?

Soln:
Thursday
5184 = 3000*(1+20/100)^n where n is number of days taken to reach 5184.
(6/5)^n= 5184/3000=216/126= (6/5)^3
So n=3
on Tuesday , population will be 3000*6/5
on Wednesday , population will be 3000*(6/5)^2
on Thursday , population will be 3000*(6/5)^3 = 5184
